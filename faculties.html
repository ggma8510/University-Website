The Faculty Model

Why We Abolished Departments

The world's greatest challenges are not confined to a single discipline. Traditional departments create silos that inhibit the cross-pollination of ideas required to solve complex problems.

Our "Goal-Oriented Faculty" model dismantles these walls. We bring in experts in specific fields who can mentor students across various ambitious goals.

How It Works: Crafting Your Personal Faculty

You don't declare a major; you define a mission. Based on your mission, you assemble a personal faculty panel. This team becomes your dedicated faculty, guiding your unique curriculum.

An Exception: The Foundation of Context

While our model champions fluid learning, we recognize that all innovative thought is built upon a deep understanding of human context. To provide this foundational bedrock, we maintain one formal faculty:

Faculty of History & Strategic Studies

This faculty serves all students, providing the essential historical and strategic perspectives necessary to ensure their innovations are responsible and wise.
